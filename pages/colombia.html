Colombia

Why should you travel to Colombia?

Colombia is one of the world's most diverse countries with two oceans, a range of climates, energetic cities, astonishing wildlife and things to do including kayaking, rafting, rock climbing, paragliding, surfing, diving, dancing and more.

Last place that I visited... Salt Cathedral of Zipaquira

Not far from Bogota lies the salt mining capital of Colombia and in it a beautiful, underground church. An abandoned, carved-out salt mine serves as the setting of the Salt Cathedral of Zipaquira.

During the week, visitors can learn more about the salt industry and the church itself, but on Sunday the cathedral opens its doors to nearly 3,000 parishioners for traditional Mass.
